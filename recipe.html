FufuRecipe

Fufu is a dish made with yam, cassava and plantain. Fufu can be eaten with different kinds of soups. Soups like; Light soup, Palmnut soup, groundnut paste soup, etc served in a big bowl and often eaten in groups. It is the main dish of the Akans in Ghana

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Cassava
  • Plantain
  • Meat
  • Spices
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Garden Eggs
  • Turkey Berries
  • Salt

Fufu meal is one of the best main dishes in Africa, especially in Ghana. Fufu with light soup and meat.

Instructions

  1. peel yam, cassava and plantain
  2. boil till fully/completely cooked
  3. steam/cook meat in a separate pot(add all necessary spices
  4. boil tomatoes, garden eggs, turkey berries, pepper all together until soft
  5. blend tomatoes and others
  6. Add mixture to the meat on fire
  7. add salt to taste
  8. pound yam together with cassava or cassava together with plantain
